CUSTOM "KAHOOT" QUIZ

×

Leadership in Veterans United's Production department reached out to our team to see if we could come up with a better solution than Kahoot.com for their quarterly loan knowledge quizzes. They wanted something they had more control over, specifically in regards to security and reporting, so I built a custom-made Kahoot-style quiz in Articulate Storyline, complete with a dynamic scoring system, that could be uploaded and tracked in our company's LMS.

TYPE: Gamification, Block Coding

DATE: October 2021

PROJECT ATTRIBUTES: Problem Solving, Articulate Storyline, Adobe After Effects

RESULT: This course has been repeatedly, and securely, updated and launched for several quarters to VU’s Production department.

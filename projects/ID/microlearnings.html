ESSENTIAL SKILLS MICROLEARNING SERIES

In response to numerous unmet requests for basic skills courses, I started an initiative to create multiple series of micorlearnings to quickly fill those gaps in Veterans United's course offerings. One such series, was the Essential Skills Microlearnings Series which included courses on writing emails, phone etiquette, and basic grammar.

THE SIX THINKING HATS

My manager at Veterans United asked me to step in and complete this project last minute. I had two weeks to create a course on the Six Thinking Hats by Edward de Bono from the Subject Matter Expert’s PowerPoint and a basic visual theme. After finalizing a quick-turn timeline with the SME, I built the course in Articulate Rise and used Genial.ly to add interactive embeds.

TYPE: Quick-Turn Microlearning

DATE: January 2025

PROJECT ATTRIBUTES: Planning & Prioritization, Time Management, Articulate Rise, Genial.ly, Adobe Illustrator

RESULT: This course successfully launched by the original deadline.

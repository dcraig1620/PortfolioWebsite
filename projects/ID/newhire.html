NEW HIRE ORIENTATION

The first instructional design project I was solely responsible for started as a request by Veterans United’s Director of Culture to create an online version of their standard new hire orientation in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. I created a series of four Rise pages based around VU’s values designed to deploy over the course of an employees first month, along with additional physical and virtual assets to accompany them (I.e. stickers, posters, worksheets, etc.).

TYPE: Instructional Design, Standardization of Experience

DATE: April 2021

PROJECT ATTRIBUTES: Articulate Rise, Adobe Illustrator, Copy Editing

RESULT: VU’s Director of Culture loved the final product. My leadership was impressed and encouraged me to create a standardized process, for our team and other trainers throughout the company, for utilizing Articulate Rise.

WORK SAFETY SERIES

I collaborated with the Head of Safety and Security at Veterans United on a variety of animated courses on topics including: fires, gas leaks, earthquakes, severe weather, safe lifting, ergonomics, safe driving, personal protective equipment, and active shootings.

TYPE: Animation

DATE: 2022-Present

PROJECT ATTRIBUTES: Vyond, Adobe After Effects, Copy Editing, Audio Recording, Audio Editing
